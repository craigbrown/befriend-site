befriend

an app for your mental health

University can be hard. Now there's an app to help you through. befriend helps you stay on track by matching you up with a trained mentor who can talk with you about your problems.

Peer support

befriend helps you stay on track by matching you up with a trained mentor who can talk with you about your problems. These mentors are final year undergraduate and postgraduate students, and are matched with you based on their expertise in the issues you're having. They've been through an intense program of counselling training and will understand what you're going through.
